About Us

Brisa Pool Services has been providing swimming pool maintenance, repair and renovation in Palm Beach and Broward Counties. Our Company is flexible and can adapt to any clients specific needs. It is our goal to take away all your pool maintenance concerns, and make your life more relaxing and less stressful.

Our Services

Weekly Pool Service

We’ll do the cleaning and filter monitoring of your pool. We use top of the line equipment and our pool technicians are experienced and well trained.

Renovation

We specialize in in-ground Pool Renovation Service and we’re well known for the high quality work we do when it comes to all kinds of renovation.

Repair

Got damaged equipment? No problem. Just give us a call and we’ll send a professional to you as soon as we can. Our repair service is quick, affordable and reliable.

Leak Detection

We offer both the equipment and expertise required to provide not only the best pool leak detection in the area, but also repair your pool.
